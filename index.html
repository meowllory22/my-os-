yummy chocolate chip cookies <3



ingredients:

in a large bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, cornstarch, and salt.

in a seperate medium bowl, whisk butter and sugars. add egg + yolk until combined

pour small bowl into big bowl and mix. add chocolate chips!! it might not stick to dough much, but thats ok!
